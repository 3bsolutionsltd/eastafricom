About Our Company

Your trusted partner for premium East African agricultural exports

15+ Years of Agricultural Export Excellence

We are dedicated to sourcing and supplying organic products that are defect free. We work through partnerships with a variety of farmers and firms, giving us a 360-degree perspective on East African agricultural exports.

With field offices strategically located in Rukungiri and Ntungamo, we maintain direct relationships with coffee and cocoa farmers, ensuring quality control from farm to export. Our washing station in Nyamikokye/Nyakisenyi allows us to process premium coffee while maintaining the highest standards.
